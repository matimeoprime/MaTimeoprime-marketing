About Us

A diversified marketing and distribution company.

MaTimeo is a diversified marketing and distribution company with subsidiaries that specialize in the production, packaging, and distribution of quality food products and other consumer goods.

The company not only manages its own brands but also partners with businesses by providing strategic marketing and promotional services. Through this dual approach, **MaTimeo drives sales, expands market reach, and fosters sustainable business growth.**

Vision

To become a leading marketing and distribution hub, connecting quality products with consumers across local and international markets.

Mission

To deliver trusted and innovative marketing solutions that help businesses grow, ensure efficient distribution of high-quality goods, and build long-term partnerships.

Core Values

Guided by Integrity, Quality, Innovation, Partnership, and Customer Focus to ensure sustained business growth and consumer satisfaction.

Service

Specialized solutions in Branding, Printing, and Packaging to elevate your product.

Branding

Developing a compelling, cohesive brand identity, including logo design, style guides, and strategic positioning to maximize market recognition.

Printing

Professional, high-resolution printing services for labels, marketing materials, promotional items, and all necessary documentation.

Packaging

Creating effective and attractive product packaging solutions that ensure product safety, regulatory compliance, and consumer appeal.

MaTimeo Chocolate

As a diversified company, MaTimeo is deeply committed to high-quality food production through **MaTimeo Foods**. We specialize in the sourcing, production, and packaging of premium products, with a special focus on fine Ghanaian chocolate and other consumer goods.

Our 'Own Brands' division ensures meticulous quality control and innovative packaging, guaranteeing that only the best products reach the local and international markets. Discover the rich taste and quality assurance that defines our proprietary food lines.
